Who We Are

A collective born from
fog and color.

Caligo Arts Collective — Caligo SF for short — is a creative duo based in San Francisco. We make art that lives outdoors, on walls and in communities, and we extend that same creative energy into music, photography, video, and three-dimensional objects.

The name Caligo is Latin for fog. We wear our city's signature atmosphere as a badge: mysterious, layered, and always shifting. Our work embraces that same spirit — it's never static, never predictable.

We're open to mural commissions, community art projects, public space activations, music collaborations, and 3D printing explorations anywhere in the Bay Area.

Our Origin

Meet Cal.
Our first wall.

Every collective has a beginning. Ours was Cal — a great horned owl painted on a San Francisco wall that started as a sketch and became a neighborhood landmark.

Cal the Owl was the first mural we created together as Caligo Arts Collective. That wall told us something: that what we make in the fog has a way of sticking around.
